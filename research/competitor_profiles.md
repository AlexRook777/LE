# Competitor Profiles

Analysis of LinkedIn profiles with 10k+ followers in the AI / automation / consulting space.

Per the [LinkedIn Content Playbook](../linkedin%20methodology/LinkedIn%20Content%20Playbook.md):
> Go to LinkedIn and find 5 profiles that have 10k+ followers.
> Study their profile: headline, banner, links, featured, CTAs, website, funnel.
> Scroll through their posts and study the 5 most viral posts for each profile.

## Format

```markdown
### [Profile Name]
- **URL:** [LinkedIn profile link]
- **Followers:** [count]
- **Headline:** [their headline]
- **Niche:** [their specific focus area]
- **What works:** [what you like about their approach]
- **What doesn't:** [what you'd avoid]
- **Top viral posts:** [links and brief analysis of structure/hook/topic]
```
