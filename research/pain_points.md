# Pain Points

Pain points collected from intro calls, social selling conversations, and market research.

These are the real problems that potential clients experience — and the raw material for high-converting content.

## Format

```markdown
### [Pain Point]
- **Source:** [call, conversation, research, etc.]
- **Frequency:** [how often this comes up]
- **Who has it:** [role/company type]
- **Current workaround:** [what they do today]
- **Quote:** [actual words if available]
- **Post potential:** [which category × perspective fits this best]
```
